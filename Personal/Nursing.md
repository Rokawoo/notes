# Nursing Lecture Notes - Lecture 1
### Mark Klimek

## I. ABG Interpretation
* Same direction (pH ↑ bicarb ↑ OR pH ↓ bicarb ↓) = **Metabolic**
* Opposite directions (pH ↑ bicarb ↓ OR pH ↓ bicarb ↑) = **Respiratory**
* pH determines: acidosis (<7.35) vs alkalosis (>7.45)

**Normal Values:**
* pH: 7.35-7.45
* Bicarb: 22-26 mEq/L ("222 to 6")

## II. Assessment Scales
**0-4 Scale:**
* 0-1: Hypo
* 2: Normal
* 3-4: Hyper

## III. Vocabulary
**GI Assessment:**
* Borborygmi = ↑ bowel sounds
* Obtundant = ↑ one more step
* * Obtundant Lethargy =  more lethargic
