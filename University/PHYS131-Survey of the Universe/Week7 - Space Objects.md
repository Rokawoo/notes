# Comets

Dirty balls of ices (methane, ammonia, CO_2, & H_2O) left over from the formation of the solar system (leftover outer-solar system planetesimals)

## Parts of a Comet

- Nucleus: Solid Ice / Rock Core
- Coma: Halo of vaporized gasses and dust from nucleus, reflected light from sun
- Ion / Plasma tail: Made up of very light charged particles and **points directly away from the Sun**. Tells of nothing of the motion, but relative location to Sun.
- Dust tail: Made up of heavier dust particles, so it points partly towards the direction that the comet is coming from (but still away from the Sun).

# Meteor Showers

Meteor showers occur when the Earth orbits into leftover comet debris.
These happen the same time each year, because leftover comet debris is relatively stationary.
